About Me

Hi,my name is Priya!!

And Cruchy munch is my little corner of the internet!

In this space, I am always sharing fresh, flavorful, (mostly) healthy recipes that I love to make and eat in my real, actual, every day life.
If I wouldn’t eat it in real life, I won’t put in on the blog. My goal is to inspire you with food that is both approachable AND exciting,
whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, your roommates, or your friends. I want you to be so excited about these recipes
that you eagerly await 5pm when you can go home from work and start cooking.
I like to create and share recipes that feature real good, real food made for real people.
My recipes are fresh, seasonal, mostly simple, often healthy, and sometimes peppered with a little cheese and butter mixed in.
